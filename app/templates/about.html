About Us



The information on the web is increasing day by day and with the help of cheap internet services, a large number of consumers are now relying on ecommerce sites for their daily needs. With the development in technology, we have seen a huge rise in popularity of web applications and a lot of people are now relying on these applications to buy their daily needs. But due to this rise, we have witnessed a lot of reviews on a product and for an individual it is difficult to go through all these reviews and identify whether the product is good or not. There is no site that focuses on sentiments related to a review, though they use ratings, sometimes this ratings are subjective.

Our project focuses mainly on user-reviews and pricing of that product
so that the end user can benefit from it and make the right choice.
